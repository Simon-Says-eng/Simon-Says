About Simon

Who is Simon? Simon is a guy who lives in Germany. Simon is not like everyone. Simon always tries to think different. Every day, I am asking myself, what do you do the whole day and what should you do. Most of my time i am a guy who edits and fills forms for offices and office people. In the other quarter of my day i try to develop electronics or software. I started my career in this branch with 15 years as an electronics technician. Already in the beginning of my career i saw more solutions in a problem than anyone else. So, after three years of education and one year of work i studied electronics. Since this day i got through different firms and sometimes developed important circuit diagram and sometimes huge crap. But I’ve always done it my way.

Why this Blog

Like i said above, i see many things different than other people. I have often Ideas which most of the people would name as crazy. But i want to share my ideas. Maybe there are intelligent people out there which can solve some of the topics. Maybe there is the next Iphone in one of my ideas and it needs just a person with more IQ to develop it. Also, i want to improve my English skills in writing. Maybe someday i write a book and it does not harm to train this skill a bit.
